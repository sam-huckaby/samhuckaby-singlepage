About Me+
If you came here because you want to know who I am, then this section is for you! I am not a sum of the parts you will find below, although it is often tempting (even for me) to consider myself that. I am first and foremost a sinner saved by grace. Like you, I am an image bearer of God, custom-built to reflect His glory and majesty in much the same way that a statue or a painting reflect the majesty of their subject. And yet the world is broken, and us with it, anyone can see that is the case. Left to our own devices we run as far away from truth and goodness as we can because we run from God, the source of truth and justice. But even still God loves us. And it is because of that love that He sent His Son to die in my place - and yours.

So I come to you as your friend. I love you because God first loved you, just like He first loved me. Since you will likely never know me any way other than via the internet, I care for you as my friend by taking you along on my adventures of learning and exploration. One day I may be live-streaming a build of an OCaml auth library and the next I might be interviewing you or someone you know about their origin story! So let's learn together! Follow me on X or Twitch or YouTube to hear more about what I'm up to, or look for me at a conference and say "hi", I would love to get to know you.
